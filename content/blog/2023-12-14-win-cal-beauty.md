![Windows 3.1 running in DOSBox with the Calendar application open.](wincal.gif)

I've been collecting a lot of the games and applications I played on my old MS-DOS/Win 3.1 computer from the Internet Archive. Coming back to Windows 3.1 as an adult with "all the things I know" now about computers that I didn't then, I've been poking around the UI. It's functional but ugly, unlike System 7 from the Macintosh days (which I despised at the time).

Oddly, the application that stuck out the most was the bundled "Calendar" within the "Accessories" folder. [I've been on a plain text kick]() and the calendar app is perfection. I'm _almost_ tempted to re-create this by parsing my [calendar.txt]() file..
