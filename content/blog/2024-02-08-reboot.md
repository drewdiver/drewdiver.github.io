This site has gone through many changes. The original idea was to use it as a blog, but I have eclectic tastes and my own opinions that I don't want bleeding into what is more of a landing area to get in touch or glance at what I'm up to.

Using my name feels like I'm trying to create a brand and I'm definitely **not** a brand.

Therefore, I am deciding to keep this space mostly static and use the blog function to post occasional updates of music or other projects (both personal and professional) I've finished and am ready to share. I'm also retaining the [reading log](/reading) and "[now](/now)" section.
