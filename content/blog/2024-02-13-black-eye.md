This simple menu bar app was written entirely in SwiftUI and prevents the Mac from both sleep and screensaver using `IOKit`.

A similar app I used recently decided to go behind a paywall. The challenge was accepted and using my [Hacking with macOS](https://www.hackingwithswift.com/store/hacking-with-macos) book, the [Apple Developer Documentation](https://developer.apple.com/documentation) and ChatGPT (😏) I managed to hammer out [Black Eye](https://github.com/drewdiver/Black-Eye) in an evening.

The project is completely open and released under the [MIT license](https://mit-license.org/).

Happy hacking!
