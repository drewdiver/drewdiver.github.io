 There's an artist by the name of [Michiru Aoyama](https://michiruaoyama.bandcamp.com/) who basically releases one album _per week_ and sometimes one every two days! I've often thought about doing something like this, a "stream of conscious" collection of recordings, sort of like [morning pages](https://juliacameronlive.com/basic-tools/morning-pages/) but in the form of audio to cultivate a discipline and set deadlines to hone my craft and experiment.
