While poking at the calendar on Windows 3.1, I started wondering if System 7 had a similar bundled application. Alas, my System 7 emulator does not. I did manage to find a disk image called simply "[Calendar](https://minivmac.github.io/gryphel-mirror/c/sw/general/calendar/index.html)" by David Oster and Mike Schuster from 1986 that I installed. I was even more shocked to see he has a descendent for "OS X" (Apple Silicon even!) called [Daytz](http://www.turbozen.com/mac/daytz/index.html).

Though Daytz is super simple and plain (well, _rich_ text), it forces its own folder and file structure. I'm quite happy with my `calendar.txt` and the way [Vimwiki]() handles diary entries.
